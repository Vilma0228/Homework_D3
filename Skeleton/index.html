Correlation Between Poverty and Smoking

We used the 2014 survey data from the American Community Survey (ACS) and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) to explore the relationship between a demographic factor (Poverty), and a health risk (Smoking). The calculated correlation between Poverty and Smoking is 0.52 (positive). Each marker represents an individual state in the United States. Hover over the markers to see details for each State!
